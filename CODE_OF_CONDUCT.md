# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:
- respectful and constructive feedback
- empathy for different experience levels
- focusing criticism on ideas, not people

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

To report Code of Conduct violations privately, email **maintainers@spingiantcrm.com** with:
- a description of what happened
- links/screenshots/logs that help establish context
- whether there is an immediate safety concern

Reports are handled confidentially and reviewed by project maintainers. We aim to acknowledge reports within 72 hours and provide a status update within 7 days.
